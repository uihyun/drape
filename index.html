drape — Digital Closet, Virtual Try-On, and OOTD Calendar

drape turns the clothes you already own into a digital closet you can browse, try on virtually, and log day by day. Snap a piece on the hanger or while you're wearing it — drape crops out everything but the garment, auto-tags it (category, colour, season, style), and files it in your wardrobe. Pick any item to virtually try it on a photo of yourself before you wear it.

What drape does

  • Digital closet: photograph each item — drape crops the garment cleanly and auto-tags category, colour, season, and style.
  • Virtual try-on: pick any item (or a full outfit) and see it on your own body photo, with your face and pose preserved.
  • Outfit builder: combine items into named outfits.
  • OOTD calendar: log what you wore each day on a month-view lookbook.
  • Community feed: follow other users, save / like / comment on looks.
  • Languages: English, Korean, Japanese.

How drape works

  1. Add an item — snap a photo. drape silently crops, auto-tags, and files it while you keep adding.
  2. Register 2–3 full-body identity reference photos on first launch.
  3. Pick any item (or build a full outfit) and tap "Try on" — drape composites it onto your reference, preserving face, body, and pose.
  4. Save the result, log it as today's OOTD, or share it to your lookbook feed.

Privacy

Your closet and identity reference photos are private to your account by default. Outfits and OOTD entries are private unless you choose to publish them to the community feed.
