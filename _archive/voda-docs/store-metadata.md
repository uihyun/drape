### Description (4000 char max)
archelier (ar-shə-LYAY) is a portmanteau of *Architect* and *Atelier* — your architect's atelier in your pocket. It turns a photo of your space into a photorealistic redesign in seconds. Pick a style, set your budget tier, and get back a fully styled space — every furniture piece labeled, with shopping links, color palette, and lighting plan.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Upload up to 3 photos of the same room — AI keeps consistency across angles
• Choose from 12 styles: Modern, Scandinavian, IKEA-style, Japandi, Industrial, Coastal, Classic, Art Deco, Mid-Century Modern, Maximalist, Vintage, Bohemian — or write your own
• Pick a budget level: Budget / Mid / Luxury — finishes adjust to match
• Empty room mode: stage a vacant room from scratch (great for real estate)
• Edit specific regions: replace a sofa, change a wall color, swap a rug — all by typing what you want
• Sketch / floor plan / 3D model also work — AI turns them into photoreal renders
• Exterior + garden modes: redesign facades, balconies, yards
• AI design chat: ask follow-up questions about your generated room
• Shop the look: every furniture item has a search link to Naver Shopping (Korean) or Google Shopping
• Save to collections, share to friends, like designs in the public feed
• Custom styles: save your own style descriptions for reuse

PLANS
• Free: 2 free designs as a guest, +3 on sign-in, +1 daily login bonus (cap 10), +5 per friend invited
• Pro: unlimited generations, 4K downloads without watermark, priority feed, 3-day free trial

WORKS ON ALL YOUR DEVICES
• iOS, Android, and web — your designs sync across all platforms

PRIVACY
We don't track you across other apps. Your photos stay private to your account unless you publish them.

## App Review Information Notes (ASC → 앱 정보 → App Review 정보 → 메모)

ASC 의 App Review 메모는 **영구** — 한 번 적어두면 다음 빌드들의 심사 때도 리뷰어가 계속 보게 됨. 흔히 받는 거부 (5.1.1 account deletion, 1.2 UGC moderation, sign-in flow 등) 의 안내를 미리 박아두면 재제출 시 매번 같은 질문 안 받음.

### Reuse 가이드 (다음 앱)
- 섹션 순서: AUTH → IAP → ACCOUNT DELETION → UGC → PRIVACY (계정 lifecycle 묶음 → 콘텐츠 정책 → 개인정보).
